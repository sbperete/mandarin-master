1. Introduction

Welcome to Mandarin Master! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web services.

By using Mandarin Master, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

🛡️ Our Commitment: We will never sell your personal data to third parties. Your learning journey is private and secure.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you sign up using Google, Facebook, or Apple, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture
  • Learning Data: Your study progress, vocabulary words learned, quiz scores, study streaks, and time spent learning
  • Feedback & Support: Information you provide when contacting customer support or submitting feedback
  • User Preferences: Your selected learning level, notification settings, and app customizations

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Features you use, buttons you click, pages you visit, and time spent on each activity
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, unique device identifiers, IP address
  • Performance Data: App crashes, error logs, and performance metrics to improve stability
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (not precise GPS location)

2.3 Payment Information

We use PayPal for payment processing. We do not store your credit card details, banking information, or full PayPal credentials on our servers. PayPal handles all payment data securely according to PCI-DSS standards.

We only store: subscription status (active/cancelled), subscription start date, and PayPal transaction IDs for verification purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve our Chinese learning platform
  • Personalization: To customize your learning experience based on your level and progress
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, authenticate logins, and verify subscriptions
  • Payment Processing: To process subscription payments and manage billing
  • Communication: To send you important updates, subscription confirmations, and customer support responses
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our app and identify areas for improvement
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service
📧 Marketing Communications: We may send you promotional emails about new features or special offers. You can opt-out anytime by clicking "unsubscribe" in any email.

4. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We only share your data with third parties in the following limited circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

  • PayPal: For secure payment processing and subscription management
  • Cloud Hosting: AWS/Cloudflare for secure data storage and content delivery (encrypted)
  • Authentication: Google, Facebook, Apple for OAuth sign-in services
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage statistics (no personally identifiable information)
  • Email Service: For sending account notifications and support responses

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as:

  • In response to valid legal requests (subpoenas, court orders)
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • To investigate fraud or security issues
  • To comply with regulatory obligations

4.3 Business Transfers

If Mandarin Master is acquired by or merged with another company, your information may be transferred to the new owners. We will notify you via email before any such transfer occurs.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Storage Security: Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Access Controls: Strict employee access limitations with multi-factor authentication required
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Secure Authentication: OAuth 2.0 for third-party logins, password hashing for email accounts
  • Payment Security: PCI-DSS compliant payment processing through PayPal
⚠️ Important: While we use industry-standard security measures, no system is 100% secure. Please use a strong, unique password and enable two-factor authentication where available.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

6.1 Access & Portability

  • View Your Data: Request a copy of all personal information we have about you
  • Export Your Progress: Download your learning history, scores, and vocabulary lists

6.2 Correction & Deletion

  • Update Information: Correct inaccurate or outdated personal data
  • Delete Account: Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data

6.3 Control & Consent

  • Opt-Out of Marketing: Unsubscribe from promotional emails (account notifications still sent)
  • Limit Data Processing: Restrict how we use your data for analytics
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke previously granted permissions at any time

6.4 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@mandarinmaster.app with your request. We will respond within 30 days.

🌍 Regional Rights:
• EU/EEA Users: GDPR rights including data portability and right to be forgotten
• California Users: CCPA rights including sale opt-out (we don't sell data)
• UK Users: UK GDPR protections

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Mandarin Master is suitable for all ages, including children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

For Users Under 13:

  • Parental consent is required before creating an account
  • We collect minimal information: name, learning progress, and subscription status
  • Parents can review, delete, or request their child's data at any time
  • We do not show targeted advertising to children
  • No public chat features or social sharing for under-13 accounts

Parental Controls:

Parents can contact us at parents@mandarinmaster.app to:

  • Review what information we have collected from their child
  • Request deletion of their child's account and data
  • Refuse further collection or use of their child's information

8. International Data Transfers

Mandarin Master is operated from Malawi, but our servers and service providers may be located in various countries, including the United States and European Union.

By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country of residence. We ensure that all international transfers comply with applicable data protection regulations through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
  • Ensuring adequate data protection safeguards are in place
  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest

9. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

9.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login, session management, and core functionality (cannot be disabled)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings, language choice, and learning level
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand app usage and improve features (can be disabled)
  • Authentication Cookies: Keep you logged in across sessions

9.2 Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save your learning progress offline and provide a smoother experience. This data never leaves your device unless you sync across devices.

9.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect app functionality.

10. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Cancelled Subscriptions: Learning progress saved for 2 years in case you return
  • Deleted Accounts: All personal data deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Payment Records: Transaction history retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Anonymous Analytics: Aggregated usage data may be retained indefinitely (no personal identifiers)

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors.

When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • For significant changes, we will notify you via email or in-app notification
  • Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
