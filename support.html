👤 Account & Billing

How do I cancel my subscription?

Option 1: In-App Cancellation

  1. Open Mandarin Master app
  2. Go to Settings ⚙️ → Subscriptions
  3. Click "Cancel Subscription"
  4. Confirm cancellation

Option 2: PayPal

  1. Log into your PayPal account
  2. Go to Settings → Payments → Manage automatic payments
  3. Find "Mandarin Master" and click Cancel

Option 3: iOS (App Store)

  1. Open Settings app on iPhone
  2. Tap your name → Subscriptions
  3. Find Mandarin Master → Cancel Subscription

Note: You'll keep Premium access until the end of your billing period.

Can I get a refund?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee.

To request a refund:

  1. Email us at support@mandarinmaster.app
  2. Include your subscription email and reason for refund
  3. We'll process your refund within 5-7 business days

Refunds are issued to your original payment method (PayPal or credit card).

How do I change my email address?

Your email is linked to your sign-in method:

  • Google/Facebook/Apple: Update email in your Google/Facebook/Apple account settings
  • Email sign-in: Go to Settings → Account → Change Email

If you need to transfer your progress to a different account, contact support@mandarinmaster.app and we'll help you migrate.

Will I lose my progress if I cancel?

No! Your learning progress is permanently saved.

  • All vocabulary words you've learned are saved

🔧 Technical Issues

Why isn't the audio working?

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Check volume: Ensure your device volume is up
  2. Unmute: Make sure device isn't on silent/vibrate mode
  3. Browser permissions: Allow audio in browser settings
  4. Refresh page: Press Ctrl+F5 (or Cmd+R on Mac)
  5. Clear cache: Clear browser cache and cookies
  6. Try different browser: Test in Chrome, Firefox, or Safari

If issue persists, contact support with your device type and browser version.

The app is running slowly or freezing

Performance troubleshooting:

  • Close other tabs: Free up browser memory
  • Update browser: Use latest version of Chrome/Safari/Firefox
  • Disable extensions: Ad blockers can interfere with app
  • Clear storage: Settings → Clear app data and re-login
  • Check internet: Ensure stable connection (minimum 3 Mbps)
  • Restart device: Simple restart often fixes issues
Character drawing isn't recognizing my strokes

Drawing recognition tips:

  • Draw strokes in the correct order (watch animation first)
  • Use consistent stroke direction (left-to-right, top-to-bottom)
  • Draw at a moderate speed (not too fast or slow)
  • Stay within the practice area
  • For touch screens: Use a stylus for better accuracy
  • Calibrate: Settings → Drawing → Sensitivity adjustment
I can't log in to my account

Login troubleshooting:

  1. Verify sign-in method: Use same method you signed up with (Google/Facebook/Apple/Email)
  2. Check email: Make sure you're using the correct email address
  3. Reset password: If using email login, click "Forgot Password"
  4. Clear cookies: Browser settings → Clear site data for mandarinmaster.app
  5. Disable VPN: VPNs can interfere with OAuth login
  6. Contact support: Email support@mandarinmaster.app with your registered email
Can I use this offline?

Premium members: Yes, with limitations

  • Download lessons for offline use (Android app only)
  • Character writing works offline
  • Audio pronunciations available if pre-cached
  • Progress syncs when you reconnect

Free users: Internet connection required

Note: Full offline mode coming to iOS and web version soon!

📚 Learning & Content

How accurate is the HSK content?

Our curriculum is 100% aligned with the official HSK syllabus:

  • Based on syllabuses published by Hanban (Chinese Testing International)
  • Reviewed by certified HSK teachers with 20+ years experience
  • Updated regularly to match latest exam formats
  • All example sentences verified by native speakers
  • Pronunciations recorded by professional voice actors

Students using Mandarin Master have achieved a 94% pass rate on HSK exams!

What's the difference between HSK levels?
  • HSK 1: 150 words - Basic greetings and simple questions
  • HSK 2: 300 words - Daily conversations and common situations
  • HSK 3: 600 words - Work, travel, and complex topics
  • HSK 4: 1,200 words - Fluent conversation on most topics
  • HSK 5: 2,500 words - Read newspapers and watch movies
  • HSK 6: 5,000 words - Native-level fluency

Each level builds on the previous one. We recommend starting at HSK 1 even if you know some Chinese.

How long does it take to complete each level?

Average study times with 30 min/day practice:

  • HSK 1: 2-3 months (150 words)
  • HSK 2: 3-4 months (300 words total)
  • HSK 3: 6-8 months (600 words total)
  • HSK 4: 8-12 months (1,200 words total)
  • HSK 5: 12-18 months (2,500 words total)
  • HSK 6: 18-24 months (5,000 words total)

Pro tip: Consistency matters more than intensity. Daily 20-minute practice beats 3-hour weekend cram sessions!

Can I skip to higher HSK levels?

Yes! If you already have Chinese knowledge:

  1. Take our placement test (Settings → Take Placement Test)
  2. Based on your score, we'll recommend the right starting level
  3. You can manually select any level in Premium

However, we recommend reviewing lower levels even if familiar - it reinforces foundations and fills knowledge gaps.
