## Platform-Specific Notes

### Microsoft Store
- Package: MSIX via PWABuilder
- Revenue: 100% yours (own PayPal commerce)
- Fee: FREE to publish
- Action: https://www.pwabuilder.com → enter URL → download Windows package → submit to Partner Center

### Samsung Galaxy Store
- Submit as PWA (email pwasupport@samsung.com)
- Revenue: 100% yours (own PayPal commerce)
- Fee: FREE
- Action: Email with app URL + screenshots + description

### Huawei AppGallery
- Submit TWA APK (same as Google Play)
- Revenue: 80/20 split for education apps
- Fee: FREE to publish
- Action: https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/en/appgallery

### Google Play Store
- Submit TWA AAB (signed)
- Revenue: 85/15 split (first $1M at 15%)
- Fee: $25 one-time (already paid)
- Needs: assetlinks.json with signing fingerprint

### Amazon Appstore
- Submit same APK as Google Play
- Revenue: 80/20 split
- Fee: FREE (subscription already active)
- Action: https://developer.amazon.com/apps-and-games
